About Us

We're building best-in-class navigation software to power the next generation of autonomous robots.

Our Mission

At 1N Robotics, we believe every robot should navigate intelligently and autonomously, regardless of its form or application. Our mission is to accelerate robotics innovation by providing production-ready navigation software that works across all platforms—from drones and ground robots to quadrupeds and humanoids.

Building reliable autonomous navigation is one of the hardest challenges in robotics. Most companies spend years developing custom navigation stacks for each platform, duplicating effort and slowing innovation. StarNav solves this—one unified platform that deploys once and scales everywhere.

StarNav combines cutting-edge AI with robust software engineering to deliver navigation that just works. Our platform continuously improves through AI updates, making every robot in your fleet smarter over time—no engineering required.
