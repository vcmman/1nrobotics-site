The 1N-Core Platform

The unified hardware, software, and cloud solution for building and deploying intelligent robots.

1N-Core Hardware

A reference design for a powerful, compact, and energy-efficient autonomy box. It includes a high-performance processor, a suite of sensors, and a variety of I/O ports. You can build it yourself or work with one of our manufacturing partners.

1N-Core OS

A real-time operating system for robotics, built on top of a hardened Linux kernel. It provides a rich set of libraries and tools for perception, planning, and control. The OS is designed to be modular and extensible, so you can easily add your own custom algorithms.

1N-Core Cloud

A cloud-based platform for managing your fleet of robots. It allows you to deploy new software, monitor the health of your robots, and collect and analyze data from the field. The cloud platform also includes a powerful simulation environment, so you can test your algorithms before deploying them to the real world.
